Vision.css

Vision.css is a ligntweight CSS animation library with it's simple usage. It's easy to use just like making an omelete. It makes your headache of animating a website damn easy.

Installation

Installing Vision.css is easy. Just follow one of the below steps and you are done with installing great animation library.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://meetkalani.github.io/Vision/vision.css">

To use Vision.css just add above link tag to your webpage

Wanna try it locally? You can Download or Clone Vision.css from my Github Repository.

Usage

After Installing Vision.css. Follow below steps to get up and running cool looking animation on your website.

<div class="vision slideInLeft"><div>

To Animate your beloved website. Just add "vision" class to your element. after that choose one of the animation from our wide variety of animations and add the name of animation to your element. That's it. Now you are done with your headache of animating a website with cool looking animation.

Customization

Customization of our animation is also easy as animating it.

Duration

<div class="vision slideInLeft duration-1"><div>

Vision.css provides the following durations:

  • duration-1
  • duration-2
  • duration-3
  • duration-4
  • duration-5

To Increase duration of animation add on of the above customization's class to your element and don't forget to add "vision" class to your element

Iteration

<div class="vision slideInLeft infinite"><div>

To make your element animated for infinite times. You can add "infinite" classs to your element
